What's going on here?

This application was made to find local Chefs cooking awesome food in your neighborhood. Eventually you'll be able to request a Cheff cook some meals, or just purchase ready made meals. This is for Bakers and Chefs alike, or maybe some Breakfast connoisseurs. Who knows?

Safety is important

Chefs will have inital inspections and will not be allowed to have pets, they will also be required to use common sanitary practices, including thermometers, timers, hold times, and disinfection practices.
